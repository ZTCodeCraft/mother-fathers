About us

A big-small coffee place in the center of Sofia

We believe in three things: obsessively good coffee, food that makes you close your eyes for a second, and the kind of atmosphere where strangers become regulars.

From homebaked 24% butter croissants and Greek-inspired focaccia to specialty Freddo Cappuccinos — everything we do is a little strange and a little unusual. Just the way we like it.
